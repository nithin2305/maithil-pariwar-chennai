About Mithila Culture

Mithila, also known as Tirhut, is a historically and culturally rich region located in the Indian state of Bihar and parts of Nepal. It is renowned for its knowledge, art, literature, and traditional values.

ЁЯЧгя╕П Language & Literature

The primary language here is Maithili, one of the oldest languages in India. Renowned poets like Vidyapati and Kokilashwar Jha have enriched its literary legacy.

ЁЯОи Art & Aesthetics

Mithila is globally famous for its Madhubani paintings, a traditional art form inspired by mythology, deities, nature, and rural life.

ЁЯЩП Traditions & Festivals

Mithila's wedding customs are widely celebrated, especially the Saurath Sabha. Other major festivals include Chhath Puja, Sama-Chakeva, and Jijiya.

ЁЯСХ Clothing & Cuisine

Men typically wear dhoti-kurta while women wear sarees. Favorite dishes include litti-chokha, tarua, khaja, and dishes made from makhana.
